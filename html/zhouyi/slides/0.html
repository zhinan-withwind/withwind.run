学易之源

The Origin of I Ching

既然要学易经，当然要从头学起，而易经的源头在哪里呢？那就不得不聊一聊『无极』了。

Since we want to learn the I Ching, we should start from the beginning. Where is the source of the I Ching? We must talk about 'Wuji' (The Ultimate of Non-being).

一部电影

A Movie

每次说起『无极』这个词，我脑子里最先浮现出来的永远不是什么高深的哲学概念，而是陈凯歌导演在2005年拍的一部电影。

Whenever I mention the word 'Wuji', what first comes to mind is never some profound philosophical concept, but a movie directed by Chen Kaige in 2005.

时隔近二十年，再次观看时发现，除了夸张的色彩和搞笑的台词以外，这部片子想传达的东西还是有些内涵的——那种无始无终，周而复始的状态。

Nearly twenty years later, watching it again, I found that aside from the exaggerated colors and funny lines, the film still has some depth in what it wants to convey - that state of endless cycles, without beginning or end.

道德经中的无极

Wuji in Tao Te Ching

知其白，守其黑，为天下式。
为天下式，常德不忒，复归于无极。
—— 老子《道德经》
Know the white, keep to the black,
be a pattern for the world.
Being a pattern for the world,
eternal virtue will not depart,
return to the state of Wuji.
—— Laozi, Tao Te Ching

老子所说的无极，是一个状态，当知其白，守其黑，就可以回到无极的状态。

The Wuji that Laozi speaks of is a state. When one knows the white and keeps to the black, one can return to the state of Wuji.

逍遥游中的无极

Wuji in Zhuangzi's Free and Easy Wandering

无极之外，复无极也。
—— 庄子《逍遥游》
Beyond Wuji, there is still Wuji.
—— Zhuangzi, Free and Easy Wandering

庄子说：无极之外，还是无极，说明无极是没有边界的。

Zhuangzi said: Beyond Wuji, there is still Wuji, indicating that Wuji has no boundaries.

这让人想起宇宙 —— 其大无外，其小无内。

This reminds us of the universe - so great that nothing exists beyond it, so small that nothing exists within it.

太极图说

Explanation of the Diagram of the Supreme Ultimate

无极而太极。太极动而生阳，动极而静，静而生阴，静极复动。
一动一静，互为其根。分阴分阳，两仪立焉。
—— 周敦颐《太极图说》
Wuji and yet Taiji. Taiji moves and generates yang;
when its activity reaches its limit, it becomes tranquil.
Through tranquility Taiji generates yin.
When tranquility reaches its limit, activity begins again.
—— Zhou Dunyi, Explanation of the Diagram of the Supreme Ultimate

无极而太极

Wuji and yet Taiji

无极：从字面理解，就是没有极点，没有道理。

Wuji: Literally understood, it means no ultimate point, no principle.

太极：从字面理解，就是至高的极点，至高无上的道理。

Taiji: Literally understood, it means the supreme ultimate point, the supreme principle.

《太极图说》里用的是：『无极而太极』，并不是『无极生太极』，这告诉我们太极不是无极演化来的，无极和太极是一不是二，是同一个东西的不同状态。

The Explanation uses 'Wuji and yet Taiji', not 'Wuji generates Taiji'. This tells us that Taiji is not evolved from Wuji; Wuji and Taiji are one, not two - they are different states of the same thing.

一动一静

One Movement, One Stillness

静的时候就是无极，什么也没有；动的时候就是太极，呈现出至高无上的道理。

When still, it is Wuji, with nothing there; when moving, it is Taiji, manifesting the supreme principle.

无极是不动的，稳定的，恒定的；而太极是运动的，转化的，流转的。

Wuji is motionless, stable, and constant; while Taiji is moving, transforming, and flowing.

就像盘古的传说——当盘古睡着，就是无极；等盘古醒了，就是太极。

Like the legend of Pangu - when Pangu sleeps, it is Wuji; when Pangu awakens, it is Taiji.

混沌与鸿蒙

Chaos and Primordial Vastness

传说中天地未判，阴阳未分时，大神盘古居住在一个蛋里，这个蛋就是混沌。盘古用巨斧劈开鸿蒙，才有了阴阳，清者为天，浊者为地。

Legend says that before heaven and earth were separated, before yin and yang were divided, the great god Pangu lived in an egg - this egg was chaos. Pangu split the primordial vastness with a giant axe, creating yin and yang: the clear became heaven, the turbid became earth.

鸿蒙更多是指那个时代，混沌更多是指那个状态，而这就是无极。

Primordial vastness refers more to that era, chaos refers more to that state - and this is Wuji.

无极的本质

The Essence of Wuji

无极是宇宙和世界最初始的状态，它啥也不是，却是所有一切最原始的起点。

Wuji is the most primordial state of the universe and world. It is nothing, yet it is the most original starting point of everything.

它是宇宙大爆炸前的奇点，是空，什么也没有，却包含了万物；没有任何道理，却孕育着天下最至高的公理。

It is the singularity before the Big Bang, the void that contains nothing yet encompasses all things; it has no principle yet nurtures the most supreme axiom under heaven.

这就是无极——只可名，不可道。

This is Wuji - it can only be named, but cannot be spoken.
